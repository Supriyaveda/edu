ELECTRICAL AND ELECTRONICS ENGINEERING

The Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering was established in 2007-2008. The department offers a four year degree program in Electrical and Electronics Engineering with an annual intake of 60 students and increased to 120 students in 2012. The department also offers a two year M.Tech program in Electrical Power Systems with an annual intake of 30 students and Three Years Diploma (DEE) Course with an annual intake of 60 Students. ELECTRONICS AND COMMUNICATION ENGINEERING

Electronics & Communication Department was established in the academic year 2001-02 with an intake of 60 and increased to 180 regular and 36 lateral entry student The department has good infrastructure with different labs namely Electronic Devices and Circuits lab, IC & HDL Lab, Analog Communications Lab, Digital Communications Lab, Microwave Engineering Lab, Electronics Circuits & Simulation Lab, Basic Simulation Lab, Digital Signal Processing Lab, Microprocessors and Micro controllers Lab, Embedded Systems Lab. All the labs are fully equipped to cater the needs of the students

CIVIL ENGINEERING

civil Engineering Department was established in the academic year 2013-14 with an intake of 60 and increased to 120. It is endowed with well qualified, experienced and dedicated faculty and committed supporting staff. Students of the department are highly enthusiastic and actively participate in various co-curricular and extra-curricular activities apart from giving their best in academics. A congenial atmosphere prevails in the department and in particular between the teacher and the taught. All the laboratories of the department are well equipped with advanced and sophisticated instruments

MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

Mechanical Department was established in the academic year 2010-11 with an intake of 60 and 12 lateral entry students. The department has 20 well qualified, experienced and dedicated faculty and 4 Non-Teaching faculty, having specialization in diversified areas of technology. The Department has laboratories such as workshop, Thermal Engineering Lab, Metallurgy & Instrumentation Lab, Heat Transfer, Machine Tools & Metrology Lab, CAD/CAM Lab, Fluid Machines & Hydraulic Machines Lab and Mechanics of Solids Lab.

COMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING

computer science Department was established in the academic year 2010-11 with an intake of 60 and 12 lateral entry students. The department has 20 well qualified, experienced and dedicated faculty and 4 Non-Teaching faculty, having specialization in diversified areas of technology. The Department has laboratories such as workshop, Thermal Engineering Lab, Metallurgy & Instrumentation Lab, Heat Transfer, Machine Tools & Metrology Lab, CAD/CAM Lab, Fluid Machines & Hydraulic Machines Lab and Mechanics of Solids Lab. All the labs are fully supported with 24-hours power supply. The department consists of 3 well-furnished classrooms for theory classes and 1 Fully Equipped Seminar Hall. Apart from Central library the Dept. is Providing Library facility to the students with huge amount of volumes and Number of National and International level Journals.
